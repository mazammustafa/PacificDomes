Welcome to Pacific Domes

The Original Geodesic Dome Manufacturer

Why Pacific Domes?

As the original, award-winning manufacturer of geodesic domes, we are committed to the most efficient and elegant architectural solutions. Since 1980, we have designed and engineered the strongest, most portable, easy to install, and durable Dome Homes, Event Domes, Greenhouse Domes and Playground Domes.

We are a family owned and operated company based out of Ashland, Oregon. Our selection of geodesic domes are used all over the world for personal, corporate, and community purposes. Each dome type can be customized, offering an unlimited potential for creativity. There are also many uses of geodesic domes.

Award Winning Dome Company

We of offer many types of dome uses from Dome Homes to Event Domes and Projection Theaters

We are honored to have been recognized multiple times by the IFAI. We have received awards for excellence in both the Tent Manufacturing and Party Tent Rental categories, as well as an outstanding achievement award for Commercial Tent Rental.

Expanding our reach around the world

From Canada to Australia, you can explore our map of resort, Airbnb and glamping domes.

FAQs

How long does it take to set up a dome?
The setup time can vary depending on the size and complexity of the dome. However, our process is streamlined for efficiency, allowing you to start your business as quickly as possible.
What financing options are available?
We offer a range of financing options to suit different budgets. During your consultation, our team will discuss the best financial plan tailored to your needs.
What about permitting? Do I need a permit to set up a dome?
Permit requirements can vary by location and intended use of the dome. We recommend consulting with local authorities for specific permitting needs. Our team can provide guidance and documentation to assist you in the permitting process.
What kind of support can I expect after my dome is set up?
We provide ongoing support to ensure your dome maintains its quality and functionality. Whether it’s maintenance tips or additional accessories, our team is here to assist you.
